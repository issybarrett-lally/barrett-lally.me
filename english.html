Hi. My name is Issy Barrett-Lally. I grew in Sheffield and Glasgow, UK.

I give English lesssons to pupils of all levels face-to-face in Paris or via Skype.
I am experienced teaching high schol and middle school students, younger learners and adult learners seeking a variety of needs and interests.
My background is in foreign langauges and English literature, haivng studied these subject to degree level and worked as a translator for the Sorbonne English>French and French>Enlgish.

Classes can be on a weekly basis or for specified time period and can either take place in the 14th arrondissement or at a convenient location for you.
My hourly fee is €20/ £18 an hour.
I put the pupil at the centre of experience and strive to provide the type of tutition that best serves your learning style.
