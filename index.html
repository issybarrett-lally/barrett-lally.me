Hi. My name is Issy Barrett-Lally.

I go to the University of Glasgow, Scotland. Currently interning in Paris, France.
I am an emerging writer passionate about the screen and stage.
I care about using creativity as a tool to inspire possitive personal and social impact.

I enjoy being active, especially road cycling and skateboarding.
I like teaching people languages and editing, reading all kinds of fiction, and traveling to new places.

I'm passionate about empowering young people with art and languages. I was part of doing this at The Writing Squad.
In 2018 @ CFBWriters I co-founded a writers group for residents and friends of the Cité Internationale Universitaire de Paris.
September 2018-present @ Université Paris 1 Panthéon-Sorbonne I translate publicity material French>Engilsh for the International Relations Department.

Raised in Sheffield and Glasgow, UK, welcome to my corner of the web.
